About

I am a freelance Front-End Developer, operating internationally.

I have over 8 years of experience in interactive / creative / marketing industries, and for the last year and half, I have worked as a Front-End Developer for VisitScotland.

Working with VisitScotland has given me the opportunity to further expand my digital brain cells, which allowed me to perfect my HTML, CSS, Javascript/jQuery skills. I played a lead role in our team and that included producing the new cutting edge website which has since accumulated an enormous growth of referrals, receiving 13 million visits a year. VisitScotland utilised my time management skills, and I ensured each milestone was successfully completed and every deadline efficiently met.

Employment as Digital Designer / Email Developer with the multi-disciplinary creative agency The Union, empowered me with extensive knowledge, as well as sharpening my skills as a designer. High profile clients including Principal Hayley Group, Dalmore Distillery, Jura Whisky, Whyte & Mackay, Miller Homes have sought my expertise in marketing their campaigns.

My enthusiasm of working within this industry has granted me many opportunities to liaise with diverse projects for companies such as Peach Digital, Eastern Western Motor Group, and Edinburgh Telford College. I love what I do.

I meet challenges head on and with full commitment. Feel free to contact me if your business, organisation or charity is in need of a web based solution. I will be delighted to help.

I hope you enjoy your visit to my website!
