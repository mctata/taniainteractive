About

I specialise in designing websites and interactive applications aided by an in-depth knowledge of XHTML, CSS and W3C Standards.
I believe my job isn't just about making things look pretty. Sure, that's important but it's also about getting a message across whilst making everything as simple as possible for the user.

Originally from Portugal, I have moved to Edinburgh in 2004 where I achieved my HND Interactive Multimedia Design, and my Bsc Interactive Media Design at Napier University.
In 2008, I worked as Multimedia Designer at Edinburgh's Telford College, and I also worked as Digital Designer for a multi-disciplinary creative agency - The Union, as well as Front-End Developer for Scotland's National Tourism Organisation - VisitScotland.

I am always up for a challenge, so if your business, organisation or charity is in need of a web based solution, feel free to contact me and I will be delighted to help.
