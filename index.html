AI SaaS Product

Stepify

AI that teaches your users.

Stepify is an AI-powered SaaS platform that generates step-by-step tutorials autonomously. Give it a URL and a goal. It navigates the web application, captures real screenshots at every step, and delivers a polished tutorial with annotated images and video walkthrough. No recording. No manual work.

Documentation is one of the most neglected aspects of software. It is time-consuming, immediately outdated, and expensive to maintain. Stepify eliminates the manual work entirely using AI to autonomously browse any web application, perform each action, and capture the process in real time.

Fully autonomous: no extensions, no recording
130-point quality scoring pipeline
WCAG 2.1 AA compliant by default
SEO flywheel: tutorials become landing pages
15+ platforms supported and growing
Multi-format: web, screenshots, video

Platform

Landing Pad Website and Landing Page Builder

Professional websites without writing code.

Landing Pad Digital's core platform is a visual website and landing page builder that empowers individuals, agencies, and businesses to create professional websites without code. Available as a cloud-based solution with a free AI-powered CRO analyser that scores landing pages across UX, copy, SEO, and conversion paths.

The platform sits within a broader integrated ecosystem I've architected and maintain: analytics (Matomo), cloud storage (Nextcloud), email marketing (Listmonk), customer support (Chatwoot), business operations (Odoo), and workflow automation (n8n). All self-hosted on Docker infrastructure with nginx-proxy, automatic SSL, and comprehensive backup systems.

About

20+ years. Three countries. From developer to CEO.

I started in Portugal, moved to Scotland where I spent a decade as a front-end developer working with brands like Visit Scotland, Vue Cinemas, and Dalmore Distillery, then relocated to Chiang Mai, Thailand, where I've been based for the past ten years.

In Thailand, I spent six years at Chiang Mai Lanna Business Services (CLBS) as Web Development Manager and Team Lead, collaborating closely with ebuero in Germany to deliver web and desktop-app projects across international teams. I led development processes, mentored developers, and ensured projects were delivered on strategy, on time, and within budget.

In parallel, I co-founded Landing Pad Digital, a BOI-approved tech startup building AI-powered web platforms and tools. My path has taken me from writing CSS by hand in 2004 to running a company that ships AI products in 2026. What hasn't changed is the standard I hold myself to: clean code, accessible interfaces, and technology that serves people.

Today, I lead a remote team of developers and designers, architect cloud infrastructure, build AI tools, and develop the marketing strategies that bring them to market. I'm equally comfortable in a Docker terminal, a Figma file, or a boardroom. Certified in Docker, DevOps, and CSS preprocessors (LESS and Sass), with extensive experience in Confluence, Jira, and end-to-end project delivery.
