taniainteractive - Flash

Tania specialises in designing websites and interactive applications aided by an in-depth knowledge of XHTML, CSS and W3C Standards.
She believes her job isn't just about making things look pretty. Sure, that's important but it's also about getting a message across whilst making everything as simple as possible for the user.

Originally from Portugal, Tania moved to Edinburgh in 2004 where she achieved her HND Interactive Multimedia Design, and her Bsc Interactive Media Design at Napier University.
In 2008, Tania worked as Multimedia Designer at Edinburgh's Telford College, and recently she worked as Digital Designer for The Union. Currently, Tania works as Front-End Developer for VisitScotland.

 

I am always up for a challenge, so if your business, organisation or charity is in need of a web based solution, feel free to contact me and I will be delighted to help.
