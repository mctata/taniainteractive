Accomodation

The student accommodation service is part of student services and offers advice and information on available accommodation.
The College offers access to two types of accommodation: registered lodgings and student residential accommodation.
We occasionally receive information from private landlords about flats and advertise these on their behalf. Supply varies, and flats which become available are taken very quickly.

Student services can also give you confidential advice on housing problems, and can help you with any difficulties you may encounter during your time at the College.

Contact

Accommodation service drop-in times are available from student services (situated in the hub).
To receive detailed information on accommodation e-mail: accommodation@ed-coll.ac.uk or telephone 0131 559 4251.
