Volunteer Service

If you are interested in education and feel that you could help others to make the most of a learning environment, then why not consider volunteering at Edinburgh's Telford College?

We are looking for enthusiastic and committed individuals who can spare a few hours a week to support students in a variety of settings:
  • One to one mentoring support for new and vulnerable students
  • Be a study buddy and help build someoneâ€™s confidence
  • Befriend a student with a disability and
  • Help them integrate into student life
  • Be a classroom volunteer to support students with learning disabilities
  • Work with students to help develop I.T skills to aid study
  • Be a volunteer driver/escort for student field trips
Volunteers make a vital contribution to the organisations they support. Volunteering at the College will offer you:
  • The chance to be part of a busy, dynamic Further Education College
  • The opportunity to support students to achieve their full potential
  • Appropriate induction, training and supervision
  • Access to College facilities such as the learning resource centre and college restaurant
  • Access to appropriate training opportunities for personal development
  • The chance to make a difference
