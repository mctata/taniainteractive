Spiritual Care

The spiritual care team are happy to meet students of faith and non-faith. The team is formed of faith representatives and aims to:
  • Provide guidance, encouragement and support to students and staff attending Edinburghâ€™s Telford College
  • Provide a safe space for discussion on any issue
  • Provide direction to other sources of support (where appropriate)
  • Consider and express the diversity existing within the college community
  • Celebrate events in the multi-faith calendar, including “one world” events
Contact

Members of the spiritual care team provide a drop-in service in the faith room, on the ground floor.
For information please collect a drop-in sheet from student services, or you can access the spiritual care team by e-mail: spiritualcare@ed-coll.ac.uk.

If you have any questions about accessing the spiritual care team, you can contact student services on 0131 559 4207
