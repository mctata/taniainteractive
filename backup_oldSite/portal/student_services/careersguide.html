Careers Advisers can help you at all stages of your education with impartial and professional advice.

Visit the Careers Advisor before you come to the College for advice on the right career for you.
The Careers Advisor can also offer help when you are about to leave the College, including help with your CV, interview skills, information on job opportunities and progression onto further study at college or university.

Careers Scotland offer drop-in sessions or appointments throughout the year.

Contact

To visit the Careers Advisor, the drop-in and appointment times are available from student services (situated in the hub).
You can e-mail to receive a careers appointment at: careers@ed-coll.ac.uk or phone to receive information on our careers service on 0131 559 4243
