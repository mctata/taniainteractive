Childcare

Waterfront Nurseries provide childcare at the College for children aged three months to five years. The nursery offers high quality childcare to students and staff, with additional private places also available.

Student services allocate places for studentsâ€™ children at the nursery. You will be allocated a provisional place, which is finalised once student services receive your course timetable, offer of place letter and application for funding (with all the supporting evidence) and your eligibility for funding has been confirmed.

Bursary funded students may apply for help with childcare costs (applications are available from student services).
Full-time HN Students can apply for additional support from SAAS.

Contact

To receive detailed information on childcare, please visit student services (located in the hub), e-mail: mail@ed-coll.ac.uk or phone 0131 559 4000.
For a childcare funding application call 0131 559 4212
