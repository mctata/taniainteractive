Personal Matters, Advice and Referral

The college has a Personal Matters, Advice and Referral Drop-In service, which offers a safe environment to anyone who would like someone to talk to about any concerns they have.

If you are not sure, where to get the support you need, or in fact what support you need, this service will help you.

Help, support and guidance will be provided to anyone wishing to contact external organisations offering counselling and other related support.

Drop- in times for this service

Mon, Wed & Fri 11.30 -13.00

Tues 13.00 - 14.30

Thurs 14.30 - 16.00

Contact

Alternatively you can contact Puja Mahindru (Student Services Officer) on 0131 559 4098 or email puja.mahindru@ed-coll.ac.uk so she can arrange an appointment to meet you.
