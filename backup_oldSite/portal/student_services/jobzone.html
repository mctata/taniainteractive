JobZone

Jobzone Plus at the waterfront is a free service for students who are looking for part-time employment to supplement their income while studying.
Jobzone Plus aims to promote local job opportunities and runs a service similar to the Job Centre model.

Students registering with the service will receive a login and password to access the Jobzone Plus at website.

Once you are registered you can search job opportunities and find useful information and links to other websites. You will also receive weekly e-mail alerts to vacancies and recruitment events happening in and around Edinburgh.

Contact

In addition Jobzone@etc offers a daily drop-in during term time. Alternatively, call 0131 559 4869 for more information.
